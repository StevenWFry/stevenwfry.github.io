Name
IC4Workout — privacy policy
Last updated: March 16, 2026
Description

IC4Workout does not run analytics, ads, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Bluetooth Data

IC4Workout reads workout data broadcast by your bike over Bluetooth FTMS and Heart Rate Service characteristics. This may include metrics such as power, cadence, speed, heart rate, and workout state.

This Bluetooth data is used only to operate the app on your device.

On-Device Storage

Settings such as power correction, heart-rate zones, theme preference, and rider profile values are stored locally on your device.

Workout calculations such as elapsed time, estimated distance, and estimated calories are also performed locally on-device.

Apple Health

If you grant permission, IC4Workout can write completed workouts to Apple Health. Health data access is controlled by iOS and can be changed later in the Health app or system settings.

IC4Workout does not send your Health data to the developer.

Data Sharing

IC4Workout does not sell your data, rent your data, or transmit your ride data to developer-controlled servers.
